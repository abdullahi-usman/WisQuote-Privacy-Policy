WisQuotes Privacy Policy.


WisQuotes does not use or transport any of your personal data whatsoever from your devices.
In an advert that you may know see some permission such as READ_PHONE_STATE, these are permmissions
required by third party software(s) in order to act accordingly to the way the work, such as to provide
rich content according to usage.

There are permission such as WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE and READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE. These are permission required
only for backward compatibility on android devices < API 14 which may however be seen on devices higher than 
that API. Further more WisQuotes fetched content such as images and Quotes that are stored only on the app's
private directory supplied by the Android OS.


This policy may be subjected to modification at any time.


Thank you for your understanding.
